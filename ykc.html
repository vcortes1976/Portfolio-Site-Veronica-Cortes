DISCOVER

Yoga Kind Collective is a non-profit group of yoga teachers offering a variety of yoga classes. Our inspiring teachers boast a variety of teaching styles and life experiences, with class offerings to suit everyone from beginners to experienced yogis.

As co-founder of the Collective, I am developing a brand and community for our teachers and students. Our key values are:

  • Inclusivity - Yoga is for everybody; we strive to make our offerings accessible to everyone.
  • Openness - Through community and connection, especially during these difficult times.
  • Holistic - Yoga is the union of mind, body and spirit.

My role:

  • Market research and UX analysis
  • Gathering requirements
  • Usability testing and analysis
  • App design
  • Logo design and style guide

Project duration:

Approximately 4 weeks.

DEFINE

Key objectives:

  • Design a mobile app for iOS and Android devices.
  • Brand awareness.
  • Schedule and promote classes.
  • Give users the freedom to book/cancel classes easily wherever they are.
  • Reward users with relevant content i.e. guided meditations, podcasts, videos, daily inspirations, blog posts etc.
  • Build a community.

Style Guide

Logo

The logo represents the Collective’s three key values:

Inclusivity - The colour palette represents the energy levels of the 7 Chakras and symbolises inclusivity. The icon in the centre of the logo represents a genderless yogi sitting in lotus pose, a cross legged meditation pose.

Openness - The white background offers a sense of peace, calm, equality, openness and growth.

Holistic - The word ‘yoga’ in sanskrit means ‘union’. Yoga is the practice of mind, body and spirit. The circular shape represents this union.
